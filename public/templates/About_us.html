About Us

Welcome to YallaDo, your smart School Task Assignment system.

We are a team of passionate students who developed YallaDo. Our goal is to solve a real problem faced by many schools like the inefficient and paper-based way of assigning and tracking tasks between administration and teachers.

Our Mission:
To digitalize the communication between school administration and teachers. We provide an innovative, easy-to-use platform that makes task distribution faster, clearer, and more organized allowing teachers to focus more on teaching and less on administrative paperwork.

What Do We Offer?

  • For School Admins: A comprehensive dashboard to easily create, assign, edit, delete, and track tasks given to teachers.
  • For Teachers: A clean and simple interface to view their assigned tasks, filter them by priority, mark them as completed, and stay organized.

Why YallaDo?

  • Simple and intuitive user interface
  • Real-time task tracking and updates
  • Significantly reduces paperwork and saves time
  • Built with modern web technologies
  • Designed specifically to meet school needs
